What is Attorney Online?

Attorney Online is a project to create a multiplayer Ace Attorney experience. It features custom servers, cases, characters, music, sound effects and much much more. The only limit is really what you want to be here, as you can create your own server with your own customized content and enjoy a rich community filled with people of different interests, as AO stretches further than just the Ace Attorney universe.
