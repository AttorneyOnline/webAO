# Menu Theme Customization Ideas

Brainstorm dump for webAO menu theme customization, ordered roughly from **most revolutionary** (paradigm-shifting, never-been-done, possibly cursed) to **least revolutionary** (classic, tasteful, safe). Existing themes (`default`, `dark`, `light`, `classic`, `cyber`, `danganronpa`, `umineko`, `trilogy`, etc.) are starting reference points, not limits.

---

## TIER S — REVOLUTIONARY (1–60)
*Things no AO client has ever done. Some are physically possible, some are aspirational, all are interesting.*

1. **Adaptive AI Theme** — local model watches your usage patterns and silently re-tunes spacing, contrast, color palette to your habits over weeks.
2. **Biometric Pulse Theme** — webcam reads micro-blood-flow on your face; menu accents pulse to your heart rate.
3. **Voice-Reactive Theme** — UI hue shifts with the pitch of your mic; angry shouting tints the menu red, calm whispers go cool.
4. **Ambient Light Theme** — webcam samples room lighting; the menu shifts brightness/warmth to match the room you're sitting in.
5. **Weather-Sync Theme** — pulls live local weather; raining outside = rain on menu, sunny = sun glare on glass panels.
6. **Time-Dilation Theme** — menu visually warps the longer you've been in session (subtle bend after 1hr, full fisheye after 6hr).
7. **Quantum Superposition Theme** — multiple themes layered translucent until you hover; observation collapses to one.
8. **Generative Theme-Per-Session** — a one-shot LLM prompt at startup invents a never-before-seen theme just for that session.
9. **Procedural Lore Theme** — the menu has flavor text that tells a different micro-story each open ("Day 47 in the courthouse basement…").
10. **WebGL Liquid Theme** — menus are floating bubbles in a real-time fluid simulation; you "scoop" options with the cursor.
11. **Particle-Assembly Theme** — menus don't render, they assemble from drifting particles, then dissolve when closed.
12. **Memory-Palace 3D Theme** — menu options live as objects in a small 3D room; you spatially remember "settings is the bookshelf, mute is the lamp."
13. **Dream-Logic Theme** — menu items occasionally swap labels, drift, or develop new options that vanish on close. Subtle, never breaks function.
14. **Glitch-Reality Theme** — fake debug overlays, fake error toasts, intentional reality breaks; everything still works.
15. **ARG Theme** — hidden ciphers in the menu unlock secret palettes, fake Easter-egg "developer rooms."
16. **Ouija Theme** — choose menu items by physics-based planchette that drifts toward your hover.
17. **Tarot Theme** — instead of a list, draw a card to reveal an option; "fate-based" navigation.
18. **Synesthesia Theme** — every UI sound also paints color; every color also chimes a note.
19. **Living-Ecosystem Theme** — menu has a tiny plant/creature that grows from your usage; ignore it and it wilts.
20. **Personality-Mirror Theme** — picks colors from the AO character you play most.
21. **Temporal-Echo Theme** — ghost outlines of your last 5 cursor paths fade behind the cursor.
22. **4D Hypercube Theme** — submenus rotate through a tesseract-projection animation; visually impossible-feeling.
23. **Infinite-Fractal Theme** — zoom into any submenu reveals a self-similar deeper layer.
24. **AR Courtroom Theme** — phone build only: camera feed behind menu, fake holograph defendants standing in your living room.
25. **Bullet-Time Theme** — opening any menu briefly slows the world, dramatic camera dolly, then resumes.
26. **Cellular-Automata Theme** — menu background is Conway's Life seeded by your username's hash.
27. **Chess-Match Theme** — navigate by playing a 4-move minigame against a tiny AI; lose and the menu trolls you.
28. **Rhythm-Game Theme** — menu confirms only on the beat of currently playing BGM.
29. **Speedrun Theme** — tracks your fastest path through every menu and pre-highlights it.
30. **Crowd-Vote Theme** — opt-in: other webAO users vote in real time on your accent color.
31. **Stock-Ticker Theme** — colors track $AAPL or a ticker of your choice; green up days are green menus.
32. **Crypto-Volatility Theme** — animation speed ties to BTC volatility (calm market = slow, chaotic = jittery).
33. **Accelerometer-Sync Theme** — phone tilt physically rolls the menu; drop your phone, the menu falls.
34. **Heartbeat Pulse Theme** — phone PPG sensor or fitness-band integration; the whole UI throbs to your heart.
35. **Breath Theme** — mic detects breathing; the menu inhales and exhales with you.
36. **Sun-Position Shadow Theme** — uses your timezone & lat/long to cast realistic sun shadows on UI elements that move all day.
37. **Migration Theme** — menu items physically slide a few pixels per week to new positions; in 6 months your UI is rearranged.
38. **Decay Theme** — features you never use visually rust, peel, fade. Use them and they restore.
39. **Symbiote Theme** — the menu is a Tamagotchi; feed it usage, it grows new buttons.
40. **Head-Tracking Holo Theme** — webcam parallax: tilt your head, the menu has real depth.
41. **Eye-Tracking Theme** — webcam-based gaze; whatever you stare at expands.
42. **Sentiment-Weather Theme** — chat sentiment in current courtroom drives menu mood (heated debate = stormy menu).
43. **Defendant-POV Theme** — menu warps based on the in-character emotional state of your AO character.
44. **Evidence-Locker UI** — every menu is a wooden drawer you literally pull open with a drag.
45. **Court-Record Folder UI** — menus are case files you flip pages through.
46. **Witness-Stand Tutorial** — an NPC appears at a tiny stand and explains each menu option as testimony.
47. **Cross-Examination Help** — every menu item has a "Press" button that reveals a deeper hint.
48. **OBJECTION! Voice-Nav** — yell "OBJECTION" / "HOLD IT" / "TAKE THAT" into the mic to navigate.
49. **Cinematic Visual-Novel Menu** — every settings change plays as a 3-second VN cutscene with portraits.
50. **Logic-Chess Menu (Edgeworth)** — settings categories arrayed as a logic-chess board you traverse.
51. **Investigation-Mode Menu** — pan a courtroom scene; click hotspots for menu options.
52. **Psyche-Lock Settings** — locked features visually chained; "unlock" them by toggling related options.
53. **Mood Matrix Settings** — settings panel literally shows the Mood Matrix UI from DD.
54. **Revisualization Theme** — like Apollo's bracelet: hover any element to "perceive" hidden tooltips.
55. **Thought-Route Theme** — menu shows a branching thought tree like SoJ's Divination Séance.
56. **DNA-Helix Settings** — settings spiral as a double helix you scroll vertically through.
57. **Court-Reporter Live-Caption Theme** — every UI action types itself onto a bottom transcript bar.
58. **Replay-Mode Theme** — menu records every click and lets you scrub backward to undo.
59. **Two-Player Theme Sync** — pair with a friend; both your menus stay color-synced like couples' rings.
60. **Generative Soundscape Theme** — menu doesn't just look like a forest, it generates ambient forest audio in real time.
